Features

  • Collaboration

    Mind mapping with others has never been easier. Collaboratively, work on your next big idea with your entire team in real time.

  • Apple Intelligence

    Private on-device AI Brainstorming, Text Generation, Summarization and Image Background Removal.

  • Themes

    Make your idea shine with our splendid themes. Start with a theme that fits your thoughts from our gallery, and adjust it to create your own style.

  • Dark & Light Mode

    Keep working on your mind map as long as you need without straining your eyes.

  • Stickers

    We've designed 250+ stickers, to help you add clarity to your mind map. Every sticker can adjust to fit the color of your branch and is easy to find with Sticker Search.

  • Visual Tags

    Categorize and add more context to your thoughts with Visual Tags. Prioritize ideas and bring focus to a related group of ideas that are otherwise not connected.

  • Connections

    Collect and manage the ins and outs of your idea

  • Folding

    When your structure gets more complex, it can be super helpful to hide parts of your mind map that you might not need to see right now but want to come back to later.
